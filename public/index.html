About MUST Internet Problem Solver

This system helps students, faculty, and staff at Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST) report and track internet connectivity issues across campus.

Features:

  • Report internet problems with detailed descriptions
  • Track problem status and resolution progress
  • Prioritize issues based on severity
  • Location-based problem tracking
  • Real-time status updates

How to Use:

  1. Navigate to the "Report Problem" section
  2. Fill in all required details about the internet issue
  3. Submit your report
  4. Check the "Home" section to track your problem's status

For urgent issues, please also contact the IT department directly.
